  1. Staff Room Coordinator
    The Staff Room Coordinator will be in charge of all the equipment for the conference in the staff room, checking in and out equipment before and after committee sessions and printing resolutions. This is a very demanding and time-managing job!
  2. Page Coordinator
    The Page Coordinator will need to work closely with the USG Committees to ensure each committee room has enough pages for each committee session during the conference. They will also organize all the pages and create timetables to ensure each session is covered.
  3. Delegate Resource Center Coordinator
    This person is in charge of the Delegate Resource Centre (DRC) during the conference. The job entails money handling, selling t-shirts, providing information to the delegates and managing the room.
  4. Gala Coordinator
    The Gala Coordinator is in charge of the SSUNS Gala on Saturday night. Their job is to organize all appropriate staff and work with the Chief of Staff and Chief of Operations.
  5. Opening/Closing Ceremonies Coordinator
    The OC/CC Coordinator is in charge of setting up any preparations needed for OC/CC. The job entails preparing programs, distributing programs, slideshows, lighting, sound, set-up and tear down.
  6. Environmental Coordinator
    The Environmental Coordinator will help SSUNS reduce its carbon footprint, along with promoting the conference's carbon offset program for travelling schools.

For dedicated individuals that require less time commitment, we have pages, environmental, staff room, opening/closing ceremonies and gala staff positions available as well. Logistical staffers assist their respective coordinators in executing required tasks.

If any of these positions interests you, please apply! We welcome experts as well as newcomers to Model UN. Please fill out the application, stating your committee and position preferences. The application is due Tuesday, September 27th at 6:00pm.
